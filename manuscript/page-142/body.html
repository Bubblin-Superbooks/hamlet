POLONIUS. [Behind.] O, I am slain! [Falls and dies.]

QUEEN. O me, what hast thou done? HAMLET. Nay, I know not. is it the King? [Draws forth Polonius.]

QUEEN. O what a rash and bloody deed is this! HAMLET. A bloody deed. Almost as bad, good mother, As kill a king and marry with his brother. QUEEN. As kill a king? HAMLET. Ay, lady, ’twas my word.— [To Polonius.] Thou wretched, rash, intruding fool, farewell! I took thee for thy better. Take thy fortune, Thou find’st to be too busy is some danger.— Leave wringing of your hands.
