Act I

Scene I. Elsinore. A Platform Before The Castle.

Enter Francisco and Barnardo, two sentinels.

BARNARDO. Who’s there? FRANCISCO. Nay, answer me. Stand and unfold yourself. BARNARDO. Long live the King! FRANCISCO. Barnardo? BARNARDO. He. FRANCISCO. You come most carefully upon your hour.
