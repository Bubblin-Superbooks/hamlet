Stay’d it long? HORATIO. While one with moderate haste might tell a hundred. MARCELLUS and BARNARDO. Longer, longer. HORATIO. Not when I saw’t. HAMLET. His beard was grizzled, no? HORATIO. It was, as I have seen it in his life, A sable silver’d. HAMLET. I will watch tonight; Perchance ’twill walk again. HORATIO. I warrant you it will. HAMLET. If it assume my noble father’s person, I’ll speak to it, though hell itself should gape And bid me hold my peace. I pray you all, If you have hitherto conceal’d this sight, Let it be tenable in your silence still; And whatsoever else shall hap tonight, Give it an understanding, but no tongue.
