Enter Hamlet, reading.

QUEEN. But look where sadly the poor wretch comes reading. POLONIUS. Away, I do beseech you, both away I’ll board him presently. O, give me leave. [Exeunt King, Queen and Attendants.]

How does my good Lord Hamlet?

HAMLET. Well, God-a-mercy. POLONIUS. Do you know me, my lord? HAMLET. Excellent well. You’re a fishmonger. POLONIUS. Not I, my lord. HAMLET. Then I would you were so honest a man. POLONIUS. Honest, my lord? HAMLET. Ay sir, to be honest, as this world goes, is to be one man picked out of ten thousand.
