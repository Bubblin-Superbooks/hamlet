POLONIUS. Come, sirs. HAMLET. Follow him, friends. We’ll hear a play tomorrow. [Exeunt Polonius with all the Players but the First.]

Dost thou hear me, old friend? Can you play The Murder of Gonzago? FIRST PLAYER. Ay, my lord. HAMLET. We’ll ha’t tomorrow night. You could for a need study a speech of some dozen or sixteen lines, which I would set down and insert in’t, could you not?

FIRST PLAYER. Ay, my lord. HAMLET. Very well. Follow that lord, and look you mock him not. [Exit First Player.]

[To Rosencrantz and Guildenstern] My good friends, I’ll leave you till night. You are welcome to Elsinore.

ROSENCRANTZ. Good my lord. [Exeunt Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.]
