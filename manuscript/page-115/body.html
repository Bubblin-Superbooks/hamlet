I prythee, when thou see’st that act a-foot, Even with the very comment of thy soul Observe mine uncle. If his occulted guilt Do not itself unkennel in one speech, It is a damned ghost that we have seen; And my imaginations are as foul As Vulcan’s stithy. Give him heedful note; For I mine eyes will rivet to his face; And after we will both our judgments join In censure of his seeming. HORATIO. Well, my lord. If he steal aught the whilst this play is playing, And scape detecting, I will pay the theft. HAMLET. They are coming to the play. I must be idle. Get you a place. Danish march. A flourish.
