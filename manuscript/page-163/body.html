Scene Iv. A Plain In Denmark.

Enter Fortinbras and Forces marching.

FORTINBRAS. Go, Captain, from me greet the Danish king. Tell him that by his license, Fortinbras Craves the conveyance of a promis’d march Over his kingdom. You know the rendezvous. If that his Majesty would aught with us, We shall express our duty in his eye; And let him know so. CAPTAIN. I will do’t, my lord. FORTINBRAS. Go softly on. [Exeunt all but the Captain.]

Enter Hamlet, Rosencrantz, Guildenstern &c.
