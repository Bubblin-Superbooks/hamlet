HAMLET. One. LAERTES. No. HAMLET. Judgment. OSRIC. A hit, a very palpable hit. LAERTES. Well; again. KING. Stay, give me drink. Hamlet, this pearl is thine; Here’s to thy health. [Trumpets sound, and cannon shot off within.]

Give him the cup.

HAMLET. I’ll play this bout first; set it by awhile.

[They play.]

Come. Another hit; what say you? LAERTES. A touch, a touch, I do confess. KING. Our son shall win. QUEEN. He’s fat, and scant of breath. Here, Hamlet, take my napkin, rub thy brows. The Queen carouses to thy fortune, Hamlet. HAMLET. Good madam. KING. Gertrude, do not drink. QUEEN. I will, my lord; I pray you pardon me. KING. [Aside.] It is the poison’d cup; it is too late. HAMLET. I dare not drink yet, madam.
