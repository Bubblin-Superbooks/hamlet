Act Iv

Scene I. A Room In The Castle.

Enter King, Queen, Rosencrantz and Guildenstern.

KING. There’s matter in these sighs. These profound heaves You must translate. ’tis fit we understand them. Where is your son? QUEEN. Bestow this place on us a little while. [To Rosencrantz and Guildenstern, who go out.]

Ah, my good lord, what have I seen tonight!

KING. What, Gertrude? How does Hamlet? QUEEN. Mad as the sea and wind, when both contend Which is the mightier. In his lawless fit Behind the arras
