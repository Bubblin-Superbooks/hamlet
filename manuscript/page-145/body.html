Proclaim no shame When the compulsive ardour gives the charge, Since frost itself as actively doth burn, And reason panders will. QUEEN. O Hamlet, speak no more. Thou turn’st mine eyes into my very soul, And there I see such black and grained spots As will not leave their tinct. HAMLET. Nay, but to live In the rank sweat of an enseamed bed, Stew’d in corruption, honeying and making love Over the nasty sty. QUEEN. O speak to me no more; These words like daggers enter in mine ears; No more, sweet Hamlet. HAMLET. A murderer and a villain; A slave that is not twentieth part the tithe Of your precedent lord.
